Lead & Call Assistant
for Real Estate Team

How a small team of agents turned lost leads into booked showings with AI-powered automation

Overview

A real estate team was losing warm leads due to slow follow-up and frequent missed calls, especially after open houses or on weekends. They needed a solution to capture screening and follow up with leads across voice, SMS, and email.

The Solution

Lead Follow-Up Assistant

  • Instantly replies to website inquiries, e.g., pre-approved / email
  • Sends dynamic SMS/voice replies based on lead keywords
  • Smart replies sent automatically over 7, 14, and 20-day cycles

The Solution (cont.)

Call Assistant

  • Answers calls 24/7, capturing leads after hours
  • Handles FAQs, e.g., property details, showing requests
  • Books showings directly into the agent's calendar

Auto Follow-Up — Day 7

Results

First Response Time Under 1 min
After-Hours Call Coverage ↑ 47%
Showings Scheduled ↑ 41%
Agent Hours Recovered ≈ 7 hrs/week

Why It Worked

  • Speed & Availability: No waiting for call-backs — AI followed up in real time
  • Human-Like Experience: Messages sounded natural, like a helpful assistant
  • Focus for Agents: Agents were alerted only when leads were scored or requested a showing
