Lead Qualifier for a Marketing Agency

+18% Increase in Closed Deals in 45 Days

Overview

A digital marketing agency was struggling to keep up with inbound leads across forms, ads, and campaigns. The team needed a way to instantly qualify, follow up, and route high-intent leads to the right salespeople.

The Challenge

  • Leads came in from multiple sources (forms, DMs, etc.)
  • Manual review process led to slow replies
  • Hot leads went cold before the team could respond

The Solution

  • Smart Intake: Embedded forms qualified leads in real time
  • Instant Follow-up: Personalized replies based on lead score
  • Routing: Only qualified leads sent to the sales team

Results

  • 3x faster response time
  • 62% of leads qualified automatically
  • 18% increase in closed deals
  • Sales team only worked warm leads

Why It Worked

Automating lead qualification and early follow-up helped the team capture more high-quality leads and reduced admin work for closers.
